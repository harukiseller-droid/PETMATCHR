## PART 3: RISK & CHALLENGE MITIGATION

### Critical Risks (MUST MITIGATE):

**1. LEGAL LIABILITY (Content Accuracy)**
- Mitigation: Cite only AKC, breed clubs, vet sources
- Process: Quarterly accuracy audits, veterinarian fact-checkers
- Disclaimer: "Informational only - consult vet/trainer"

**2. BREED STEREOTYPES (Ethical + Legal)**
- Mitigation: Evidence-based content, myth-busting articles
- Approach: "Socialization 80%, breed 20%" messaging
- Action: Equal space for rescue/adoption as breeders

**3. E-E-A-T SIGNALS (SEO Trust)**
- Mitigation: Author credentials, vet endorsements, breed club partnerships
- Process: Expert review schedule, transparent corrections
- Result: Improved rankings for health queries

**4. ALGORITHM DEPENDENCY**
- Mitigation: Focus long-tail keywords first (KD <25)
- Strategy: Build authority slowly through content quality
- Action: Monthly content updates = freshness signals

**5. MISINFORMATION RISK**
- Mitigation: Multiple sources per claim
- Process: Community feedback monitoring (Reddit)
- Action: Transparent citations, invite fact-checking

**6. MONETIZATION ETHICS** ⚠️ CRITICAL
- Mitigation: Mission statement: animal welfare first
- Action: Honest when breed isn't suitable
- Process: Equal promotion of rescue/adoption
- Rule: Never recommend breeds based on commission
