# Complete List of Dog Breeds in the US
## Sorted by Popularity (AKC Ranking 2024-2025)

---

## Overview

This comprehensive list ranks dog breeds by popularity according to multiple sources:
- **AKC (American Kennel Club)**: Official breed registration data (201 recognized breeds)
- **U.S. News & World Report**: Pet insurance statistics
- **Rover**: Pet owner surveys

**Note**: There is some variance between sources due to different methodologies (AKC registrations vs. insurance data vs. surveys). This list primarily uses **AKC official rankings for 2024** with notes on alternative rankings.

## NOTES

- **AKC Ranking**: Based on purebred registrations only; does not include mixed breeds, designer breeds (Goldendoodle, Labradoodle, Cockapoo), or Pit Bull variants beyond American Pit Bull Terrier
- **Year-to-Year Changes**: Can fluctuate based on popularity trends, media exposure, celebrity ownership, health/breed standards
- **Regional Variations**: Significant differences by state and region (e.g., Chihuahuas popular in Southwest, Golden/Labs in Northeast)
- **Mixed Breeds**: Now ranked #1 in some surveys (Rover), but not tracked in AKC official rankings
- **Health Concerns**: Some breeds declining due to health/welfare concerns (Bulldogs, Chow Chows)
