## Notes

- The United States has **50 states** plus Washington, D.C. (federal district)
- There are also U.S. territories including Puerto Rico, U.S. Virgin Islands, Guam, American Samoa, Northern Mariana Islands, and Marshall Islands
- Each state has numerous cities and towns; this list includes the capital, largest city, and selected major urban centers
- Population rankings and city sizes can change over time as communities grow or decline
